# Our Office

## Welcome To Our Mississauga Dental Office!

At our dental practice we focus on providing a positive experience for all patients, from young children to adults. With an emphasis on patient communication, we give you the opportunity to participate fully in the decisions about your treatment. We involve you and inform you at every point, and we welcome your questions and feedback.

At Sheridan Dental you will feel an immediate sense of confidence in the dental care you receive. From the genuine, warm welcome that greets you at each visit to the high degree of personal attention offered throughout your experience here, our commitment to providing dental care of exceptional quality is always evident. Our warm and inviting office offers comprehensive dental services to meet your needs and to help you maintain a healthier smile.

For your convenience we are happy to accept DentalCard financing program. Dentalcard offers a simple and affordable way to finance your procedure or service and allows you to immediately acquire the care you desire. The ideal alternative to writing a cheque, Dentalcard's dental financing programs ensure that you don't delay your treatment due to cost concerns by offering you a variety of financing terms with convenient monthly payments. To learn more, go to [www.dentalcard.ca](http://www.dentalcard.ca)

Sheridan Dental is conveniently located beside the Metro in the Sheridan Centre Mall. For those with limited mobility, our office is wheelchair accessible. Plenty of free parking is available.
