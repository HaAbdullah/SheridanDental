# Single Tooth Replacement

[Dental Implants](./dental-implants.md) | Single Tooth Replacement

### Advantages

Implants not only look natural, but also feel and act like strong, normal teeth. And, because they don't directly rely on neighbouring teeth for support, implants don't compromise the health of your natural teeth.

### Post-Treatment Care

Your replacement teeth need to be treated the same as your natural teeth with daily brushing, flossing and the same amount of regular check ups. Just like your natural teeth, the better you take care of your replacements, the longer they will last.
