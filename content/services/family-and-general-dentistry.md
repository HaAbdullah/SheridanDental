# Dental Services for All Ages

## Family Dental Office In Mississauga

Preventative general dentistry is essential to maintaining the health of your teeth and gums. Regular checkups also allow for early detection of potential problems and give us the chance to share oral hygiene tips and best practices. Our dental services, we can help ensure the best oral health for all members of your family. Our dentistry services include:

- White fillings
- Crown and bridge
- Porcelain veneers
- Radiation-reduced digital x-rays
- Root canals
- Scaling and root planing
- Custom sports mouth guards
- Chipped, broken, and missing teeth repair

Your check-up includes important services in addition to the cleaning and polishing of your teeth. During your visit, our staff performs a comprehensive diagnostic examination of your teeth and mouth. This exam involves a routine check for tooth decay but it also includes screening for periodontal (gum) disease, oral cancer, tooth grinding, toothbrush abrasion, and the deterioration of old crowns, bridges and fillings.
