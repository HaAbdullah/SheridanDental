# Multiple Tooth Replacement

[Dental Implants](./dental-implants.md) | Multi Tooth Replacement

### Advantages

If you are missing several teeth in a row, a few strategically placed implants can support a permanent bridge (a set of replacement teeth). Similarly, if you have lost all of your teeth, a full bridge or full denture can be permanently fixed in your mouth with a strategic number of implants.

### Post-Treatment Care

Your replacement teeth need to be treated the same as your natural teeth with daily brushing, flossing and the same amount of regular check ups. Just like your natural teeth, the better you take care of your replacements, the longer they will last.
