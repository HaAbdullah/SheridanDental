# Dental Services in Mississauga

## Enhance Your Smile and Improve Your Oral Health!

At Sheridan Dental, our goal is to help you maintain your healthy smile throughout life. We meet virtually all of your needs for dental care. Our oral service offerings range from general family dentistry to reconstructive and sedation dentistry.

Listed below is a short list of our service offering:

**Preventive Dentistry...** The goals of preventive dentistry are to educate you, identify conditions that may compromise your oral health, and provide treatment before small problems become more serious. Preventive treatments include oral hygiene instruction, oral exams, and periodontal therapy.

**Reconstructive Dentistry…** Missing teeth, severe tooth decay and problems with jaw alignment can require more comprehensive treatment. Our office provides many solutions to assist with these conditions.

**Dentistry Asleep...** Sedation dentistry is a very effective solution for people who experience anxiety prior to and during their dental visits. Our dentist anaesthesiologist provides all levels of sedation and general anaesthesia to patients who are suitable to be treated in an out-patient anaesthesia clinic.

**Family Dentistry...** Regular dental check-ups are essential to good oral and overall health. At each visit we conduct a thorough cleaning, but we also check for signs of tooth decay and disease so we can detect and treat problems early. When problems do occur, we offer all suitable treatments, including fillings, root canals, and wisdom teeth extractions.
