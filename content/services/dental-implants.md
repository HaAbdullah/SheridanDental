# Dental Implants

Dental implants are a great dental solution for someone who has lost their natural teeth.

An implant is a stable, synthetic tooth root, usually made of titanium, in the shape of a post that is surgically placed into the jawbone. An implant post is a solid anchor that supports a permanent replacement tooth or aids in the retention and stability of the denture.
