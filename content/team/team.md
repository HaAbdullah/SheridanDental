# Our Team

## Our Mississauga Dental Team Welcome You!

At Sheridan Dental, each and every one of our staff members is completely committed to improving and maintaining your oral health. We are dedicated to excellence and superior patient care. Our team of professionals always strives to provide the highest quality dental treatment in a warm and welcoming environment.

### Azra — Office Manager / Certified Dental Assistant Level II

Azra not only has experience with the front desk, but she is also a Level II Dental Assistant. She graduated from the National Academy of Health and Business in 2011. Azra has been a great addition to the Sheridan Dental Team since joining in March of 2022. You will be welcomed by her warm, friendly personality as soon as you walk in! She remembers every detail about each patient and has built great relationships with them. Azra spends her free time with her husband and two teenage kids, enjoys yarn crafts, cooking and gardening.

### Brittany — Certified Dental Assistant Level II

Brittany has been a patient with Sheridan Dental since 2015 and over the years her interest in Dentistry has grown. In September of 2021, she took a leap of faith into a new and exciting career and became a Dental Assistant! She is now a graduate from the Canadian Academy of Dental Health and Community Sciences (CADH) and is a Certified Level II Dental Assistant. She is also a member of the Ontario Dental Assistants Association (ODAA).

Brittany enjoys making her co-workers laugh, meeting new patients, and educating them about their oral hygiene. She is always smiling and concerned about the welfare of others. When she is not at the office, she enjoys being in nature, going for walks, painting, and spending time with her family and friends.

### Elizabeth — Registered Dental Hygienist

Elizabeth (a.k.a Liz) graduated from the Toronto College of Dental Hygiene with Honours in 2018 and has happily worked as a Registered Dental Hygienist ever since. She has a strong passion for promoting oral health and oral hygiene education. Prior to entering the dental world, she worked as a healthcare administrator for 5 years, and even has a degree in Early Childhood Education from Ryerson University. Liz also earned a diploma in Medical Transcription during the pandemic. In her spare time, she loves being with family, friends, and cuddling with her dog Bennett. Liz also loves reading, painting, and watching movies. Liz has been with the Sheridan Dental team since September 2022.
